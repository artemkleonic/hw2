About Sergii Antonov

Sergii Antonov is a fertility and medical lawyer and the founder of REPRONOVA Agency. Trained as both a physician and an attorney, he has spent more than twenty years advising clinics, agencies, and intended parents on the legal and ethical safeguards of cross-border surrogacy and assisted reproduction.

Drawing on his unique combination of clinical insight and legal expertise, Sergii helps families build safe, transparent, and legally secure paths to parenthood. His work focuses on minimising legal risks for parents and children, ensuring compliance with regulations in multiple jurisdictions, and promoting ethical standards in the global fertility industry.

Through REPRONOVA, Sergii supports intended parents, agencies, clinics and donors worldwide, building bridges between medical practice and the law so that every child’s story begins with clarity, care and legal certainty.
